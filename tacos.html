Ingredients

You will need:

  • Boneless chicken
  • Olive oil
  • Lime juice
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Corn tortillas
  • Large tomatoes
  • Cheddar
  • Salsa
  • Sour cream

Steps

  1. Combine chicken, olive oil, lime juice in a pan over medium heat
  2. Stir in gatlic powder, onion powder and simmer until chicken is tender
  3. Warm tortillas in microwave until soft
  4. Place chiken in serving bowl once cooked
  5. Place chopped tomatoes, salsa and sour cream in serving bowls
  6. Create wrap according to preference
