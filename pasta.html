Ingredients

You will need:

  • Your choice of pasta
  • Cream
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Crushed red pepper
  • Oregano
  • Garlic Salt
  • Heavy Cream
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes
  2. Melt butter into cream in a saucpan over low heat. Add salt, pepper, garlic salt.
  3. Grate parmesan cheese and add it to the sauepan, stirring until cheese has melted and sauce has thickened
  4. Add cooked pasta into sauce.
  5. Toss until thoroughly coated
  6. Serve whilst hot
