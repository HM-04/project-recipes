Ingredients

You will need:

  • Warm water
  • Dry yeast
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Stir warm water, yeast and sugar in a mixing bowl and allow to sit for 10 minutes
  2. Stir salt into mixture and mix in flour
  3. Knead dough and add flour until dough is no longer sticky
  4. Cover with a towel and leave for an hour until dough has doubled in size
  5. Roll out dough
  6. Add sauce
  7. Add cheese of your choosing
  8. Bake in preheated oven until cheese has melted and crust is golden brown
